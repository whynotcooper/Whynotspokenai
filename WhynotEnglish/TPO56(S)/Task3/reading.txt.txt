Task Partitioning
Different species of insects have different ways of organizing the essential tasks thatneed to be completed in order for them to survive. One way of accomplishing thisprocess is through task partitioning. Insect groups that use this method divide, orpartition, their work into several different stages that are ordered and performed in afixed sequence by different groups of workers. An important benefit of this
technique is increased efficiency, since the workers performing each task arespecialized, which saves time and energy
