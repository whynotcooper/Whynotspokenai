Goal Displacement
Organizations often have a particular goal, and specific rules or procedures may becreated to help achieve that goal. However, sometimes employees focus so much onthe rules or procedures that it interferes with the organization's ability to achieve itsgoal. This is known as goal displacement A goal becomes displaced when the rules orprocedures meant to help individuals achieve a goal become more important than thegoal itself As a result, the organization may be less successful in accomplishing its goals.
