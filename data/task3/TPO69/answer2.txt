The concept of brand revival involves re-launching old products that were discontinued, mixing their classic, retro elements with contemporary improvements to suit modern tastes. In the lecture, the professor gives the example of a small car that was once beloved for its cute, sporty appearance and small size but fell out of favor as trends shifted. The company decided to revive this model, preserving its nostalgic design to attract consumers. Crucially, they enhanced the car with a more fuel-efficient engine, aligning with today's demand for eco-friendly vehicles. By merging the old charm with new technology, the revived car gained popularity, demonstrating how brand revival works in practice.
