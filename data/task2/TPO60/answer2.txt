According to the reading, the proposal aims to increase dormitory kitchen use by upgrading cookware and providing free cooking classes. The male student expresses agreement with this plan. One reason he gives is that his own attempt to use the kitchen was frustrating due to outdated items like a damaged teapot, so modernizing the equipment would address this issue. His second reason is that cooking classes would be beneficial and enjoyable; he points out that while some students are inexperienced, others have cooking expertise and could share it, fostering a communal learning environment that would likely attract more participants to the kitchens.
